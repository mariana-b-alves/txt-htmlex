About The Trilogy

The Dream Chapter is the first era in TOMORROW X TOGETHER's career.

The chapter has the concept of individuals undergoing an uncertain transition from boyhood to adolescence. This transition is further followed through by three parts.

The Dream Chapter: STAR: The first part that deals with the recognition of a friend going through the same limbo period

The Dream Chapter: MAGIC: The second part that deals with the magical moments the individuals spend together.

The Dream Chapter: ETERNITY: The third part that deals with the individuals facing the weight of reality.
